Dataset associated with: A subtropical cyclopoid copepod can increase body size and maintain exoskeleton formation under ocean acidification and warming

Data spreadsheets contain the raw data used in the study A subtropical cyclopoid copepod can increase body size and maintain exoskeleton formation under ocean acidification and warming. The data were collected to evaluate the effects of ocean acidification and warming on the growth, morphology, and exoskeleton formation of a subtropical cyclopoid copepod.
